toMOTko Online Help

toMOTko is a small application aimed at helping you to learn new vocabulary of foreign languages. It comes with a Glossary Manager allowing you to enter and manage new words and a Quiz that will keep asking you the selected words until you learn them properly.

To start using toMOTko quickly:

  • Getting Started Quickly

Otherwise, for more details, consult the specific sections :

  • Glossary Manager
  • Quiz
  • Preferences
  • Digraphs

You can also find additional information and glossaries on the home page at http://tomotko.sourceforge.net.
